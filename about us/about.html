The Important Things You Need To know About YCT Computer Engineering Departments.

DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER ENGINEERING

Computer Engineering Department is one of five departments in the School of Engineering. The department was established in November 2006 after an approval was received from N.B.T.E. in October, 2006 as a result of resource inspection visitation in May, 2006.

The commencement of the programme witnessed deployment of four academic staff from the department of Electrical/Electronic Engineering. The first set of Thirty-Four (34) students commenced lectures on December 18, 2006. The second set of the thirty two (32) students were admitted in the 2007/2008 academic year. The third and fourth sets admitted in 2008/2009 and 2009/2010 are made up of 115 and 67 students respectively.

The department began her HND programme in 2011/2012 academic session having obtained approval from NBTE another resource inspection. The department now has five academic staff and one laboratory staff.

NACOMES

National Association of Computer Engineering Students (NACOMES), YABATECH Chapter, is an association of computer engineering students.

Computer Engineering Mission and Vision

Here is our core values, mission nd vision statement.

MISSION

To encourage students to become self-motivated, problem solving individuals who can find and understand the knowledge needed to be successful in the profession...

VISION

The Computer Engineering Department is committed to continually improve its educational environment in order to develop rural youth with the strong academic and technical backgrounds...

CORE VALUES

Respect for freedom to associate with any person or group recognized by constituted authority. Regard for Discipline, Loyalty, Honesty, Integrity, Courage and generally acceptable ethical behavior...

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

The aims and Objectives of NACOMES include:

1

To promote the educational and societal advancement of members in the Association.
2

To discuss matters affecting the academic and social welfare of members of the association.
3

To associate the students of Computer Engineering with other Engineering bodies within and outside the country and in pursuance of this objective; it shall be obligatory that all members shall be members of the Nigerian Society of Engineers (NSE).
4

To create a forum for discussing technological advancement namely through organizing symposia, industrialists, technical exhibition, quizzes, lectures etc.
5

To advance computer engineering profession as well as cater for technological and educational welfare of computer engineering students towards professional competence.
6

To help in shaping the course of computer engineering technological development in Nigeria by encouraging and carrying out technical projects, offering useful and suggestions to the government and other bodies responsible for the policy making on and technology.

MEMBERSHIP

1

Membership is automatic for all registered students in the department of Computer engineering, School of Engineering, Yaba College of Technology, Yaba.
2

All financial members are entitled to all privileges befitting them.
3

Their membership proves them under all obligation(s) of the association.
LIFE MEMBERSHIP

There shall be an alumni association in which all graduates from the department of computer engineering shall be member, which makes them life members of the association.

BENEFITS OF BEING A MEMBER OF NACOMES

Our Benefits Includes:

1

All MEMBERS shall have privilege to make use of facilities provided by the association except otherwise stated in the constitution.
2

Bonafide members of the association shall have access to helpful information and details about the association.
3

Every member shall have the right to hear his/her suggestion(s) or grievance(s) and be processed.
4

All new members shall have the right to pass through an orientation programme to be organized by the association.
5

New members shall have the right to obtain a copy of the association’s constitution when duly registered.
6

All bonafide members shall have the right to vote and be voted for according to the association’s constitution.
