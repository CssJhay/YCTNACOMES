HERE ARE FEW THINGS YOU SHOULD KNOW

1

What does the Electoral system do?

This system provides a set of rules that determine how elections and referendums are conducted and how their results are determined..
2

What is Voter registration?

Voter Registration is the process of recording and compiling details such as names, department, class, matriculation number, ID cards, and photographs of students eligible to vote into the register of voters..
3

What are the requirements to register as a voter?

A voter must be a student of computer engineering, must have completed all admission processes, must have a matriculation number and school identity card...
4

How many times can a student register and vote?

A student can register and vote only once and. A student can also vote physically with our admins, certain documents are required, check our terms and policies.
5

Can a student vote for another candidtae?

A student can only vote for annother candidate with a different post.
